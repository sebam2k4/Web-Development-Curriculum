Ronnie James Dio

the Metal Legend

Tribute Page

Ronald James Padavona - better known by his stage name Ronnie James Dio, was an American singer, songwriter, and musician. He fronted and/or founded numerous groups including Elf, Rainbow, Black Sabbath, Dio, and Heaven & Hell. He is credited with popularizing the "metal horns" hand gesture in metal culture and was known for his medieval-themed lyrics. Dio possessed a powerful versatile vocal range capable of singing both hard rock and lighter ballads. - Wikipedia

History

  1. July 10, 1942 - Born in Portsmouth, New Hampshere, USA
  2. 1957 - Musical career begins as a bass player with the formation of The Vegas Kings.
  3. 1958 - Band changes name to Ronnie & The Rumblers. Dio mainly plays bass and does vocals<./li>
  4. 1958 - Band changes name again, now to Ronnie & The Redcaps.
  5. 1961 - Band changes name to Ronnie Dio And The Prophets.
  6. 1967 - current band transformed into a new band called The Electric Elves.
  7. 1968 - Band drops "Electric" from the name. Now they're called The Elves.
  8. 1972 - After changes in lineup, the band's name is changed to Elf. Dio now concentrates entirely on vocals.
  9. 1974 - Dio marries Wendy Gaxiola.
  10. 1975 - Dio's band Elf and Ritchie Blackmore form Rainbow.
  11. 1979 - Leaves Rainbow and replaces Ozzy Osbourne in Black Sabbath.
  12. 1982 - DIO band is formed by Dio and former Black Sabbath member Vinny Appice.
  13. 1990 - DIO changed members over the years, eventually leaving Dio as the only original member in this year.
  14. 1992 - Briefly returns to Black Sabbath.
  15. 2006 - Black Sabbath founder members and Dio collaborate in a new band, Heaven & Hell.
  16. Nov, 2009 - Diagnosed with Stomach Cancer.
  17. May 16, 2010 - Sadly, Dio passes away at the M.D. Anderson Cancer Center in Houston, Texas.
  18. Bands DIO and Heaven & Hell disband after Dio's death.
